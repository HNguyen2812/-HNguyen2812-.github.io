About me?
Just a normal girl that loves to dress up, and also dress down sometimes 😜
Here are some of my favorite Do's and Dont's...

Do…
  • Put on something that’s “not you” once in a while.
  • Adapt trends so that they work for you–and adapt is the key word here.
  • Add a couple of stylish, inexpensive things to your wardrobe each season.
  • Do borrow your husband’s clothes.
  • Have a stash of basics on hand: T-shirts, leggings, black socks and tights.
Don't…
  • Dress like a teenager.
  • Mix two seasons in one outfit.
  • Don’t over accessorize.
  • Cropped knickers can make you look shorter.
  • Force yourself to wear something for somebody else.
